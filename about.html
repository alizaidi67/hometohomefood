Our Mission

All of Us remember the days when our mother used too cook delicious food for us. Many of Us now miss those days; as along the journey to be successful; we have left our home, our mothers somewhere behind, so that we can have those treats only on holidays when we go back home. But what if we can get that food at our workplace. We have thousands of Mothers who cook food for their children. Ofcourse the food does not has added preservative, low quality oil, as after all its from the hands of a Mother. We Aim to bring this food at your doorstep, at your workplace, and also give mothers an opportunity to feed more children. So that you eat Healthy and progress.
